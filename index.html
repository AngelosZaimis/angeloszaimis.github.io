About

his path to the software develompent

The Beginning

Angelos Zaimis graduated from high school in 2014 in Kalamata, Greece. He studied graphic design in Athens, Greece. But he had to stop his studies and drop out. He got into an utterly different life. After that, he worked numerous jobs and was looking for his passion. What he likes to do in this life.

The Searching

In 2018, at 22 years old, he moved to Switzerland all by himself to pursue his dreams and goals and change his life for the better.He was looking for the right job to do and he was always curious for the better in life.

Finding his passion

After hard work, in 2020, he found out his passion for Software development, and he started to study computer science all by himself and taught himself coding.

Keep going

Now in the present, he still works, codes, and solves problems every day. He did numerous courses online and still does and learns. He is now looking for a company to work with the right mindset, to expand his knowledge and help the company with his skills. He intends to study Computer science at a university while working in the company. Now mainly works as a front-end developer and freelancer, but his goal is to be a full-stack developer.
